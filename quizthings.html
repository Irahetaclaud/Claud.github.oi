Hydroflask Campaign

Advetising Campaign Project

This campaign was a group project. I made the quiz (first three images) and the middle two and mock them up as well. For this campaign, the goal was to connect the brand and object to different platforms and our target audience, where students, active people, and people who haven't heard about the brand. For the aesthetic, the group agreed with going with fresh, informative, energetic, and nature themes; all of this themes target our audience.
