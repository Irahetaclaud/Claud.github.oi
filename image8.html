Yayaoi Kusama

Facebook SFMOMA Virtual Campaign Project

This project is based on making a Facebook layout publicity campaign for an artist. I choose Yayoi Kusama becuase her pumpkin sculptures are so fun to see. I based myself on circles as a theme due to Kusama's iconic circle art and the color red for her hair two things that make her identifiable. I created unity by the themes created, and fun to visually see. As well as use the Gestalt principals, such as proximity, similarity, continuation, and closure, where used.
