Nutrit

UX/UI Designx

This app was designed for people who want to improve themselves through improving eating habits. Nutrit is a personalized diet planner that connects you with certified dieticians and help you reach your goal. For these projects, personas were made, and the images shown are the logic flow in how the app would work and its functions. The inspiration for this app was the aesthetic of health, vibrant, fun, simple, and minimalistic. Demonstrating friendliness, support, and an open appetite for delicious healthy food. It's an open arm to a new lifestyle. Nutrit and its dietician staff are there to help you on your journey of self-care and health.
